AI Bug Logger Bot

Automated Bug Ticket Creation for the QA Team
LIVE IN PRODUCTION

Dear Leadership Team,

We are pleased to announce the launch of the AI Bug Logger Bot — an intelligent automation agent deployed on Google Chat that transforms how our QA team reports and tracks bugs.

Instead of spending 8-10 minutes manually creating each bug ticket in OpenProject, testers now simply send a message (with optional screenshots or video) in Google Chat and the bot creates a fully structured ticket in under 1 minute.

The Problem

  • Manual ticket creation takes 8-10 minutes per bug — filling fields, writing steps, classifying priority/type, uploading media
  • Inconsistent formatting — every tester writes differently, making tickets hard to triage and prioritize
  • Context switching — testers leave the app under test, open browser, navigate to OpenProject, fill forms
  • Missing information — testers frequently omit device info, steps to reproduce, or environment details
  • Delayed reporting — complex process means bugs are reported hours after discovery
  • Media rarely attached — manually uploading screenshots and videos adds friction, so evidence is often skipped

How It Works

Step 1 — QA Tester 0 sec
Sends a message in Google Chat
"Login button not working after OTP. Device: Samsung S23, OS: Android 15"
+ Attaches screenshot or screen recording (optional)
↓
Step 2 — AI Analysis 10-30 sec
Gemini 2.5 Flash analyzes and structures the report
Extracts: Title, Steps to Reproduce, Device, OS, Priority, Bug Type, Platform
Video frames analyzed for UI context and reproduction flow
Content screening rejects non-bug images (selfies, outdoor photos)
↓
Step 3 — Ticket Creation 5-10 sec
OpenProject ticket created automatically
Correct project (Android/iOS) selected based on device detection
Screenshots and videos attached in full HD
Priority, Bug Type, Environment auto-classified
↓
Step 4 — Confirmation < 1 min total
QA Tester receives ticket link in Google Chat
Ticket #1234 created → Direct link to OpenProject → Ready for triage

Key Benefits

⚡

Zero Friction Bug Reporting

Testers stay in Google Chat. No browser switching, no form filling, no manual uploads. Type, attach, done.

🧠

Trained on 611 Real IndiaMART Bugs

The AI understands IndiaMART terminology (BL, LMS, BMC, PDP, SOI, XMPP), classifies bug types correctly, and writes tickets in the team's exact style and format.

🎥

Video & Screenshot Understanding

Analyzes screen recordings frame-by-frame to extract reproduction steps, detects device/OS from UI elements, and identifies the exact bug moment.

🛡️

Smart Content Filtering

3-layer screening automatically rejects selfies, outdoor photos, and non-bug content — prevents junk tickets from entering the system.

📋

Complete, Standardized Tickets Every Time

Every ticket includes: Title, Actual Behavior, Expected Behavior, Steps to Reproduce, Device, OS, Environment, Priority, Bug Type — formatted consistently.

📎

Automatic Media Attachment

Screenshots and videos are uploaded to OpenProject in full HD, directly linked to the ticket. No manual upload step needed.
